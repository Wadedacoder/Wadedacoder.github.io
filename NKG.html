Nightmare King Grimm is one of the last fights in the game. It was added in the DLC " The Grimm Troupe ". He can be fought after defeating his normal version "Grimm". I love this fight as it is like a Ballroom dance which forces you to take a spotlight. Stay in the corner and his perfomance will dominate yours leading to your demise. You need to remember the steps or you will trip and fail. The music also adds to the atmosphere of it being an orchestra
